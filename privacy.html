Privacy Policy for VZ Player

Last Updated: April 24, 2025

Users are solely responsible for the content they access or share through the app. VZ Player does not encourage or support the viewing or distribution of any copyrighted content.

For more details, please refer to Google's ad policy: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

4. Children's Privacy

VZ Player is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.
